About the company

In the small town of Sonachny Zatyshok, there was an inconspicuous but special bakery called "Aromat Ranku". Its founder, a talented baker Albert, came to this place from a big city, dreaming of bringing some of the magic of baking art to the Jews. The story began when Albert opened his bakery in an old building, which once housed an old coffee shop. He preferred natural foods and traditional baking methods, which made his bread special. "Aromat Ranku" quickly won the hearts of local residents. Their favorite bread became a symbol of the community, and the from the smell of fresh bread became an integral part of the life of the city.
